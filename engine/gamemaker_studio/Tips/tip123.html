Sprite Editor

After making a selection in the sprite editor (or after pasting in a selection), you can "nudge" it into the exact position required using the cursor keys. You can also use <Shift> + cursor keys to move the selection 10 pixels in the direction of the cursor key, <Control> + cursor keys to move the selection 5 pixels, and <Shift> + <Control> + cursor keys to snap the selection to the next nearest grid position.
