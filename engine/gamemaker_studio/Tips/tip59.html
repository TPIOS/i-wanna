Android

You can bypass the 50MB upload limit on your Google Play submitted games by creating your final APK with an expansion file. An APK Expansion splits your final game into two parts, the *.apk and the expansion *.zip, with all the game assets placed in the separate expansion file in order to reduce the overall size of the initial *.apk package. When the user installs your game and runs it for the first time, this expansion file is downloaded and installed on their device.
